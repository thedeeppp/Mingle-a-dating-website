Supported Platforms and Devices

Mingle is currently available on iOS, Android, and HMS devices. Along with our mobile apps, you can visit Mingle.com to use Mingle for Web.
We also offer a lightweight version of Mingle you can take anywhere and everywhere - download Mingle Lite from the Google Play store.
Mingle currently supports iOS 14.0 and up, Android 7.0 and up, and the latest versions of all major web browsers (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge, etc.).
